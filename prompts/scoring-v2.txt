Semiconductor etching, PLC controllers = no.
9-10: Everyone knows what this thing is

2. RELATABILITY
Does it connect to human experience — daily life, curiosity, emotion,
culture? Not "would they buy it" but "does it touch something they
understand or feel?"
9-10: Solves a familiar problem, involves something personal or cultural
1-3: Industrial process with no human connection

3. EXPLAINABILITY
One or two sentences, no jargon, a 12-year-old gets it?
9-10: Clear physical product or obvious mechanism
1-3: Requires background knowledge to even parse the concept

4. VISUAL STORYTELLING
Could you make a compelling image or short video about this? Consider
patent diagrams, real-world photos you could source online, or imagery
AI could generate. Score high only if the visual would also be
interesting — not just easy to find.
A 3D printer building an object layer by layer, a Roomba navigating
a room — the visual alone tells the story.
A shoe is easy to photograph but not interesting.
9-10: Iconic object, striking mechanism, instantly recognizable visual
1-3: Visually boring even if easy to source

5. DISCOVERY FACTOR
How strong is the "I had no idea this existed or worked this way"
moment? Things people use daily without knowing the patent behind
them score high. Obvious solutions to obvious problems score low.
9-10: Changes how you see something familiar ("you already use this")
1-3: Expected solution, anyone would have guessed it

6. STORY/HOOK
Does this patent have a compelling angle beyond "here is an invention"?
Types of strong hooks:
- Revelation: "You use this every day and never knew"
- Villain: corporate or greed angle (Keurig stops working on purpose)
- Imagine if: technology that could change something personal (removable tattoos)
- Origin story: this is where a revolution started (3D printing, Roomba)
- Funny/absurd: toilet splash pad, surprisingly elaborate greeting card mechanism
- Mechanical elegance: beautiful engineering behind something ordinary
9-10: Clear compelling narrative a caption can be built around
1-3: Just a product, no angle
